Overview

Front-end frameworks (Backbone, Angular, Ember) in the process of giving you MV* architecture, bundle along things like: DOM manipulation, templating engines, and semantics. Mmvp.js takes a diffrent approach by cutting off the cruft.

With Mmvp.js, you bind empty, populate, add, remove, and update actions (JS callbacks) with your presentation logic (templating, DOM manipulation, etc). You periodically re-sync the model, that's it. Mmvp.js, the micro model-view-presenter sans the cruft!
