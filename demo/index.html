Mmvp.js

Writing AJAX for basic REST APIs shouldn't be hard. Also you shouldn't have to include heafty front-end frameworks (see Backbone, Angular, Ember) to simply trigger DOM actions based on changes in a model. So, I made Mmvp.js.

You bind empty, populate, add, remove, and update actions, which are just callbacks with your DOM manipulations. You periodically resync the model, and you're done. Mmvp is the micro model-view-presenter front-end framework. Please sans the cruft!
