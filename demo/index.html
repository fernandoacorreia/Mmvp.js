Overview

Front-end frameworks (Backbone, Angular, Ember) in the process of giving you MV* architecture, bundle along things like: DOM buttresses, templating engines, and DOM-presentantion politics. But, should you're MV* framework provide, or even know about the DOM?

With Mmvp.js, you bind empty, populate, add, remove, and update actions as JS callbacks containing your presentation logic (templating, DOM manipulation, etc) and periodically re-sync the model. That's all there is to it. Mmvp.js, the micro model-view-presenter sans the cruft!
